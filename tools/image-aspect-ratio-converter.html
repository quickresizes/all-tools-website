About this tool

This Image Aspect Ratio Converter helps you quickly fit images for social media, video thumbnails, websites, and more.

Choose a ready-made ratio or set your own custom size, then export in JPG, PNG, or WebP.

Your image stays private because everything is processed client-side with HTML5 canvas.

Features

  • Preset ratios: 1:1, 4:5, 16:9, 9:16, and 3:2.
  • Custom width and height mode for flexible output.
  • Before/after preview to compare changes clearly.
  • Download options: JPG, PNG, and WebP.
  • Mobile-friendly, dark/light mode compatible interface.
