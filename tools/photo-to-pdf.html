    How to Convert Photos to PDF

    • Upload one or multiple images using file picker or drag-and-drop.
    • Reorder images and remove any page you no longer need.
    • Select page size, orientation, margins, and image fitting behavior.
    • Click Create PDF and download your generated document.

    Features of the Photo to PDF Tool

    • Multi-image upload with JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WEBP support.
    • Instant thumbnail previews with drag-and-drop page ordering.
    • PDF customization for size, orientation, margin, and fit mode.
    • Browser-side PDF conversion powered by jsPDF for privacy and speed.
    • Progress feedback and downloadable output file information.

    Why Use This Tool

    Photo to PDF is fast, private, and easy to use. Since everything happens in your browser, your files are not uploaded to a server.

    It works great for assignments, office paperwork, receipts, portfolios, and any situation where multiple images need to become one clean PDF.
