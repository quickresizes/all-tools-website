How to Use

  1. Upload an MP4, WebM, or MOV file.
  2. Choose a size preset like 720p, 1080p, 2K, or 4K.
  3. For custom options, enter a width or height as needed.
  4. Click Resize Video and wait for processing to complete.
  5. Preview the resized video and click Download Resized Video.

Why Use This Tool

This Video Resizer gives you quick control over video dimensions without extra software.

It runs fully in your browser, keeping processing local and private.

The streamlined design works well for creators, teams, and beginners who want fast results.

Features

  • Supports MP4, WebM, and MOV uploads.
  • Preset sizing for 720p, 1080p, 2K, and 4K outputs.
  • Custom width and custom height options for flexible resizing.
  • Original and resized preview players in one responsive layout.
  • Client-side processing using HTML5 video, canvas, and MediaRecorder.
