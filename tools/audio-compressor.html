How to Compress Audio

  1. Upload your audio file in MP3, WAV, AAC, M4A, OGG, or FLAC format.
  2. Choose a compression level, then fine-tune the exact output bitrate.
  3. Click Compress Audio and wait for progress to reach 100%.
  4. Preview the compressed audio and download it instantly.

Features of the Tool

  • Client-side FFmpeg WebAssembly compression (no backend required).
  • Audio preview before and after compression.
  • Quick presets: low, medium, and high compression levels.
  • Advanced bitrate control from 320 kbps down to 64 kbps.
  • Output details including original and compressed file sizes.

Why Use This Audio Compressor

This tool keeps your files private by processing everything in your browser, making it ideal for static hosting platforms such as GitHub Pages. It is fast, simple to use, and gives you control over the quality-size balance for sharing, publishing, and storage.
