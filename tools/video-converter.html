How to Use

  1. Upload an MP4, WebM, or MOV file using the video upload field.
  2. Choose your target format, quality level, and preferred resolution.
  3. Click Convert Video and wait for processing to complete.
  4. Preview the converted output and click Download to save your file.

Why Use This Tool

Video Converter Studio is designed for speed, privacy, and convenience. Your media stays in the browser, making it ideal for quick conversions without uploading files to external servers.

Features

  • Supports MP4, WebM, and MOV input and output selections.
  • Client-side processing powered by HTML5 video, canvas, and MediaRecorder.
  • Multiple quality presets and resolution options for flexible output.
  • Live source and converted video previews with download support.
  • Responsive, professional interface that adapts to dark and light themes.
