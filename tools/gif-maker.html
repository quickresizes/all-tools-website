What this GIF Maker includes

  • Multi-image upload, drag-and-drop importing, and URL-based frame import.
  • Per-frame timing, crop, rotation, flip, brightness, contrast, and saturation controls.
  • Multiple text layers and emoji stickers with drag positioning, outline, shadow, scaling, and rotation.
  • Real-time preview with looping, play/pause controls, reverse order, and transitions.
  • Client-side GIF export with resolution presets, quality tuning, and loop count options.

Privacy and performance

This tool is designed for GitHub Pages and other static hosting platforms. Image editing and GIF rendering happen locally in your browser using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Your source images are not uploaded to MediaTools Pro servers, which makes the workflow fast, private, and easy to use on desktop and mobile devices.
