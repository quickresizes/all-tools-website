Privacy & Compatibility

  • All QR decoding runs locally in your browser. No image or scan data is uploaded to any server.
  • Works on modern desktop and mobile browsers, including camera scanning on supported HTTPS or GitHub Pages deployments.
  • Flashlight controls are shown only when the active camera and browser expose torch support.

What This Scanner Handles

  • Website URLs, plain text, email addresses, phone numbers, SMS payloads, geo/map locations, and WiFi QR codes.
  • Smart quick actions such as opening links, copying text, starting an email, calling a number, and reviewing WiFi details.
  • Responsive touch-friendly controls, dark mode compatibility, accessible labels, and auto-updating scan history with timestamps.
