How to Use

  • Choose an MP4, WebM, or MOV video file from your device.
  • Pick a compression level based on your quality and file-size needs.
  • Click Compress Video and wait for browser-side processing to complete.
  • Preview the compressed output and click the download button to save it.

Why Use This Tool

This Video Compressor runs completely in your browser, so your files stay local while processing. It is ideal for quick size reduction before sharing videos by email, chat, or social platforms.

Features

  • Supports MP4, WebM, and MOV uploads.
  • Compression levels for quality-first or size-first output.
  • Live original and compressed video previews.
  • Automatic file size comparison after processing.
  • Client-side only workflow compatible with GitHub Pages hosting.
