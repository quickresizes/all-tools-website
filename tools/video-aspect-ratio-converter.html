How to Use

  1. Upload a video file in MP4, MOV, WEBM, MKV, or AVI format.
  2. Choose one of the aspect ratio presets or select custom dimensions.
  3. Click Convert Aspect Ratio and wait for progress to reach 100%.
  4. Preview your converted video and download it to your device.

Features

  • Preset ratios: 16:9, 9:16, 1:1, 4:5, 21:9, and 3:2.
  • Custom width/height support for advanced workflows.
  • In-browser conversion with FFmpeg WebAssembly (no backend required).
  • Live progress bar, output preview, and one-click download.
  • Designed for creators publishing across multiple social platforms.

Why Use This Tool

This converter helps you quickly repurpose one video for multiple platforms without installing desktop software.

Since processing runs in your browser, your files stay on your device for improved privacy and speed.

It is ideal for creators, social media managers, and teams that need fast, format-ready video outputs.
